About GovHRPay

Empowering Human Resource and Payroll Management for Norzagaray’s Government Workforce.

Our Vision & Purpose

GovHRPay is a modernized Human Resource and Payroll Management System designed to streamline operations for local government offices and departments. With automation, secure data handling, and analytics, it supports efficiency, transparency, and faster service delivery across HR and payroll functions.

Developed for Norzagaray, Bulacan, the system integrates digital solutions that empower HR officers and employees to manage records, attendance, and payroll accurately and easily.

About Norzagaray, Bulacan

Norzagaray is a 1st class municipality located in the province of Bulacan, Philippines. It is known for its scenic landscapes, including the famous **Biyahe sa Ipo Dam**, **Angat Dam**, and **Bocaue Hills**—a blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage.

Founded in 1860, Norzagaray has evolved from a small agricultural town into a thriving community with a growing economy. Its rich history, cultural traditions, and devotion to progress reflect the spirit of the people of Bulacan.

Today, the local government of Norzagaray continues to embrace modernization by adopting digital governance tools such as GovHRPay to ensure better transparency and public service.

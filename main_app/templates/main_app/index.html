About the System

The GovHRPay is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to streamline the management of human resources and payroll processes within the Local Government of Norzagaray. It serves as an all-in-one solution for efficiently handling employee records, attendance, leave applications, and payroll computation.

This system was developed to address the challenges of manual HR and payroll operations — such as repetitive data entry, record inconsistencies, and delays in processing. By integrating automation, data security, and role-based access, the system enables a more organized, transparent, and accountable workflow for both HR officers and payroll administrators.

The HR module focuses on employee information management, attendance tracking, and performance monitoring, while the Payroll module is designed for precise salary computation, deductions, and payslip generation. Together, these modules ensure that employee records and financial transactions are consistently synchronized and error-free.

Built using modern web technologies and a secure database architecture, the GovHRPay system reflects the commitment of Norzagaray’s local government to modernization and digital transformation. It empowers public sector administrators to focus more on strategic HR initiatives and less on repetitive paperwork — ultimately improving government efficiency and employee satisfaction.

About GovHRPay Payroll

Empowering Accurate, Transparent, and Secure Payroll Management for Norzagaray’s Government Workforce.

Our Vision & Purpose

GovHRPay Payroll is a modern digital payroll system designed to ensure accurate salary computation, automated deductions, and transparent payslip generation for local government employees.

It integrates seamlessly with the HR system to provide real-time attendance-based payroll, tax and contribution automation, and secure financial records management.

About Norzagaray, Bulacan

Norzagaray is a 1st class municipality located in the province of Bulacan, Philippines. GovHRPay Payroll supports the municipality by ensuring every employee receives accurate and timely compensation.

By automating payroll, deductions, and payslips, the system strengthens transparency, accountability, and financial integrity in public service.

GovHRPay Payroll is part of the municipality’s digital governance initiative, working hand-in-hand with the HR system for complete workforce management.
